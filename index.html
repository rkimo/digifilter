Meet the digifilter app

The DigiFilter app will be available on any smartphone device. It contains many features and insights to track your usage, set custom goals and rewards, and ultimately quit your habit for good.

  • Easy access dashboard showing your current day, week, and goals.
  • Like math? Neither do we. Your data is condensed into easy to read charts.
  • Data export to .xls or .txt
  • Smart notifications. If you're getting to your set limit, we'll give you a friendly reminder
  • How much do you pay for a pack? Set the number and we'll keep track of how much money you've saved.
  • Extra features like cheats, rewards, health, and more.
